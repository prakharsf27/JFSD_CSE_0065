Aero India 2025

The biennial Aero India 2025 air show commenced in Bengaluru, focusing on enhancing India's defense manufacturing capabilities and increasing exports. Defense Minister Rajnath Singh highlighted that domestic defense production is projected to exceed ₹1.25 trillion ($14.24 billion) by the fiscal year ending in March, with exports reaching a record ₹210 billion.
